Dear PPL-Guest,

I am Van Nguyen, Director of The Propellant - a student-run organization with a vision to develop self-awareness among Vietnamese youth. I am writing this email to introduce our incoming program and hope to invite PPL-Guest to become our Ambassador for photo contest “Tớ, Mộc”.

As a part of the “SELF” event series this summer, “Tớ, Mộc” is a photo contest with the topic “What creates you?”. With the contest, The Propellant hopes to create an inclusive space the Vietnamese youth to explore self-awareness and hold meaningful dialogues with themselves and the world.

       .

 . Thank you for your consideration of collaboration with The Propellant. Have a nice day!

Sincerely,
Van Nguyen
